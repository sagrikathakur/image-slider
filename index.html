Electric Mouse Pokémon

01 / Electric

Pikachu

Pikachu loves sparks and bright adventures. It is fast, cheerful, and always ready to light up the scene. When you scroll, the environment dynamically reacts to the Pokémon's energy.

02 / Grass

Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur grows a plant bulb on its back and enjoys calm afternoons in sunny fields. It draws energy from the sun to grow stronger every single day.

03 / Fire

Charmander

Charmander has a warm tail flame and a playful spirit. Its fire shines brightest when it feels inspired, and it burns fiercely when angered or in battle.

04 / Water

Squirtle

Squirtle's shell is not merely used for protection. The shell's rounded shape and the grooves on its surface help minimize resistance in water, enabling it to swim at high speeds.
